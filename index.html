Instructions

Welcome to Trash Panda: A game all about sustainability! Complete environmental tasks from the checklist to get food, energy, and toys to keep your racoon happy. But be careful! If you don't keep up with the tasks and any of your pet's stats drop to 0, they will be very sad :(

Different tasks will give you a specified number of resources. The more difficult the task, the more resources you get. Also, different categories of tasks give different rewards. Sustainable eating nets you food, reducing waste gives toys, and recycling gives energy.
