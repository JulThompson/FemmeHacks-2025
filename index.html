Instructions

Welcome to Trash Panda: A game all about sustainability! Complete environmental tasks from the checklist to get food, energy, and toys to keep your racoon happy. But be careful! If you don't keep up with the tasks and any of your pet's stats drop to 0, they will be very sad :(

Different tasks have different values. Red tasks are worth 5 resources, blue are worth 3, and white are worth 1. The more difficult the task, the more resources you get. If you complete one red, two blue, and three white tasks, your raccoon will grow up and you will move to the next stage!
